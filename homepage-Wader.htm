Home Page of Andrew Wader

Welcome to the official website of a novelist and screenwriter. Attention! A word of caution: if you feel uneasy or hold strong reservations toward erotic content, I strongly advice you not to explore the material presented on this website.

My stories aim to improve well-being, inspire optimism, strengthen self-worth, and create uplifting visions of the future. They often draw upon alternative metaphysics and ethics, and some are designed as practical tools—helping readers overcome personal difficulties and, even more importantly, address the pressing challenges of our time.

My Perspective

I'm writing from the perspective of someone who believes that three serious errors need to be corrected.  In short:

1. It's important to recognize that the third possible worldview, expressed by Baruch Spinoza is the true one.
2. It is important to raise awareness that humanity participates in the recurring rebirth of the world — this
    is the true purpose and meaning of life.
3. Human beings are guided, in fact by B-morality.

 About My Work

I have published many academic texts under my real name, most of them peer-reviewed and interdisciplinary in character.

I have also written several popular science books.

For many years, I have also been creating fictional works that are novelistic in form and almost ready-made as film scripts, often structured around dialogues.

 Goals of This Website

 Through this official author site, I intend to:

 1. Inform readers about the publication of a novel cycle — an epic saga in the style of 'Star Wars', conceived as
    a ready-made  screenplay for a television series. I name this sequel as "The Andromeda Saga"

 2. Present other works that serve, surprisingly, as practical tools not only for enhancing well-being and health,
    but sometimes for healing through Carl Gustav Jung’s concept of sexuality.

 3. Share stories that enable imaginative journeys into the past and future, so vividly that  they may allow readers
    to perceive   themselves in new family and social roles.

 4.  Encourage readers to join a unique form of play I call the meta-game - a creative endeavors in which participants
     craft their own works  and showcase them on a personal website, where they also reveal and justify their intentions
     and purposes.
